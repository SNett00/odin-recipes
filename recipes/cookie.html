Shawn's Cookie Recipe

Recipe Description

Craving delishious fresh cookies? You've come to the right place! These gooey chocolate chips will melt right in your mouth, making you reach for more.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking soad
  • 1 cup choclate chips
  • 1 (3 ounce) bar dark chocolate
  • 3 tbsp white chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Combine brown sugar, butter, white sugar, and salt in a large bowl;mix until creamy. Add egg and vanilla;beat until mixture lightens.
  2. Mix flour and baking soad together in a seperate bowl. Add slowly to the wet ingredients until mostly incoporated. Fold in milk chocolate, dark chocolate, and white chocolate using spatula. Cover the dough and refrigerate for 30min or up to 48 hours.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) when ready to bake.
  4. Divide cold dough into 2 1/4-ounce portions and place on cookie sheet.
  5. Bake in the oven until the edges start to look golden brown and crispy, 10-12 min. Let cool, roughly 30min.
