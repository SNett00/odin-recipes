Shawn's Pizza Recipe

Recipe Description

This pizza recipe is one of a kind! Are you looking for a healthy, dairy-free pizza recipe? Then this is the place to be!

Only having a handful of fresh ingredients you can have this dish done in under an hour!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Green Peppers
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat an oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C)
  2. Roll out pizza dough
  3. Evenly spread tomato sauce on the now rolled pizza dough
  4. Spread out the additional topings on the pizza
  5. Put the pizza in the oven for 20-23 minutes
